Welcome to the Geocaching Capture The Flag (CTF) Challenge Page!

This series of caches is designed to educate geocachers in security basics, basic cryptography, cybersecurity tools, and more to come. To complete a cache, you must complete the online exercises on this site. There is a resource page with information to help you. The challenges will be beginner level, but it is recommended you complete the first geocache before attempting any of the other caches. Once you have successfully completed the exercises, you will be given a clue to where the geocache is located, not exact coordinates. Some caches in this series have a field puzzle that you will need to complete in the field that are related to the exercises you completed on the site.
